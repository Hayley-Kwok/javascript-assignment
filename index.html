Different Actions from buttons

  1. Turn Around Button:

    After clicking the button, the cat will turn around.

  2. Move to the Corner Button:

    After clicking the button, the cat will move to the bottom right corner.
    Please don't double click the button while the cat is moving.

  3. Dance Button:

    After clicking the button, the cat will have a short dance.
    Double click the button will speed up the dance.

  4. Reset Button:

    After clicking the button, everything will be reset and the cat will go back to its original state.

Different Effect of clicking on the canvas

  1. Clicking on a canvas point away from the cat will make it move to that point.
    Please click the reset button after moving when you want to have other actions from the cat.
  2. Clicking on the cat’s face will change its colour to red.
  3. Clicking on the cat’s mouth to make it meow.
